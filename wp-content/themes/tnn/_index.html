Welcome to Tir na nOg Irish Pub & Restaurant

We’ve been pulling pints & entertaining folks since 1997 when we opened as the first Irish pub in downtown Raleigh.

As in Ireland, music is an important part of our atmosphere. We have hosted several national and international legends such as, The Dublin City Ramblers, Albannach, Seven Nations, Battlefield Band, Old Blind Dogs, Teada, Grada. The Prodigals, and many more. We offer traditional Irish music every Friday night at 7pm and Irish jam sessions every Sunday. Each month we also host Irish dancing from local Irish dance schools.
